About


Web-Native is new and intends to be an open community for developers who are passionate about native languages, platform features and APIs. If that describes you, please feel free to join! Simply drop us a mail at join@web-native.dev. (A formal onboarding process is yet to be established; and, no problem if you want to help with that.)



Web-Native Tools


We develop libraries, tools, and specifications that open up new possibilities on the web, starting at the platform level. We want anyone to be able to get more done with less dependencies! Our work aims to ease-out the limitations that has historically made this difficult.


Feel free to visit our repos on Github and our documentation site. Feel free to be part of this! If you already have opensource tools that fit into our mission, you've found a home for it! We'd love to promote it! If you want to help with web-native development in some other ways, please reach out at join@web-native.dev.



Evangelism


The web platform has never been this exciting! Through articles, talks, conferences, meetups, trainings and workshops, we will be sharing just how it feels!


Feel free to be part of this! If you already have written about new possibilities on the platform, or want to help with web-native evangelism in some other ways, please reach out at join@web-native.dev.



COVID19 Support


Keeping the lights on in the dark wake of COVID19 is not a trivial thing to do! The financial weight of the Web-Native project is currently already overwhelming! We however believe in the support of the opensource community to get by. We have a Patreon campaign here where you can easily drop us a stipend. We've also worked at rewarding your kind gesture with exciting perks.
