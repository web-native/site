About


Web-Native is a new community for developers who are passionate about native languages, platform features and APIs. (This is so new that we haven't launched officially.) You're welcome in, as there's every chance that our mission is here describing the very values you care about! Meet fellow developers and other stakeholders who are passionate about making the web work for our everyday need.


Simply drop us a mail at join@web-native.dev. (While we work on a formal onboarding process. And, no problem if you want to help with that.)



Web-Native Tools


We develop libraries, tools, and specifications that bridge major gaps on our toolbox and on the web platform itself. In fact, the most of our experiments are each headed for a standards track and eventual merging with the platform. With a more empowering platform, we can all just #useThePlatform and get more done with less dependencies!


Check out our Github repos and our documentation site. Feel free to be part of this! If you already have, or are working on, opensource tools for the good of the general web, we'd love to work with you! If you, otherwise, want to help with Web-Native in some other way, we'd be more than glad.


Please reach out at join@web-native.dev.



Evangelism


The web platform has never been this exciting! Through #hashtags, articles, talks, conferences, meetups, trainings and workshops, we will be sharing the new possibilities and breakthroughs across the web that are shaping the future of the web!


Feel free to add your voice or let us know how we could work together. Simply reach out at join@web-native.dev.
